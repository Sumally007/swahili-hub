About us

Let us share with you a little bit of history.

Fonimu was founded by a group of enthusiasts who believe NLP can
revolutionize access to services in Africa. This has been the core of our
drive. Today, we are a community solving the challenging Swahili NLP
problems that will impact millions of people across Africa, as well as look
toward the future.
